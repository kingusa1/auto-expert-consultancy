About Auto Expert

Motor Claims Intelligence You Can Trust

Auto Expert is a motor claims intelligence and vehicle assessment service that delivers clear, structured insight into vehicle damage, repair costs, and claim outcomes — for both insurance professionals and vehicle owners.

We provide independent, evidence-based technical analysis free from insurer or repairer influence. Our assessments are grounded in real-world market data, repair methodology, and deep claims experience.

Whether you're an insurance broker dealing with a borderline total loss, a claims team managing high-volume periods, or a vehicle owner seeking transparency on your claim — Auto Expert provides the structured insight you need to make confident decisions.
